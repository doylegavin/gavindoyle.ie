## üí° Content Strategy Tips

1. **Be Specific:** "1,000+ users in 3 months" beats "rapid growth"
2. **Show, Don't Tell:** Screenshots, metrics, examples beat claims
3. **Tell Stories:** Case studies are more compelling than feature lists
4. **Prove It:** Testimonials, metrics, and third-party validation matter
5. **Make It Scannable:** Bullets, numbers, and short paragraphs win
6. **Update Regularly:** Add new projects, metrics, achievements as they happen
