## ğŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### Writing Photo Context:
- **Be specific:** Include names, places, dates when relevant
- **Be personal:** Write like you're telling a friend
- **Be fun:** Your tone is casual and humorous - embrace it!
- **Be brief:** 1-2 sentences is perfect (more is bonus)

### Good Context Examples:
âœ… "I'm lowkey kinda strong this boy is like 110kg"  
âœ… "Best Friends for more than half of our lives. Here's to the rest of it"  
âœ… "Me and my cousins receiving our Christmas presents on our nana's floor, 2010"

### Not-So-Good Context Examples:
âŒ "Basketball photo" (too vague)  
âŒ "Me with friends" (not specific)  
âŒ "2023" (no story)

### Taking Screenshots:
- **Resolution:** 1200x600px is ideal (but any clear screenshot works)
- **Clean up:** Close extra tabs, hide personal info
- **Capture:** Show the key features and interfaces
- **Crop:** Focus on the important parts

### Creating Blog Images:
- **Use Canva:** Easiest option with templates
- **Be consistent:** Same style across all 9 images
- **Use your photos:** Can use existing photos as backgrounds
- **Add text:** Make titles readable at small sizes
